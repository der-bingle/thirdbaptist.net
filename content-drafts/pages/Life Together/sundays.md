# Sunday Worship

Each Sunday, we gather as Third Baptist West Frankfort. Our gathering is a family gathering, and we love to have company join us. Come in comfortable clothes and with an open heart.

## What are your services like?

Sunday School begins at 9:30am, and worship begins at 10:40am.

In worship, we pray, sing, read Scripture, hear the Word preached, and respond together to the Good News of Jesus Christ.

Recent sermons and worship services are available on our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@ThirdBaptistWestFrankfort).

## Where do kids go during service?

Children are welcome in our worship gathering. More details about children's ministry and nursery options can be added here.

## Do you have Sunday School?

Yes. Sunday School begins at 9:30am.

## Where are you located?

We meet at 1100 W 6th St, West Frankfort, IL 62896.
